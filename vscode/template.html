Daily Scoop
  • ​India Government Considers Tariff Cuts to Boost Green Hydrogen Production: India is considering tariff cuts on machinery for green hydrogen production to strengthen its position in the clean energy sector. The government plans to exempt certain equipment from import levies and reduce the GST on green hydrogen, currently at 12%. These measures would apply only to export-focused production, aiming to boost the viability and competitiveness of India's green hydrogen market.
  • ​Cabinet Approves ₹2,642 Crore Rail-Road Bridge in Kashi: The new bridge will feature four railway lines and a six-lane highway, expected to save 8 crore litres of diesel annually and boost regional growth. The project aims to ease congestion and handle 27.83 MTPA of freight.
